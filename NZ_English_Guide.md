## 🍦 2. Kiwi Slang & Social Lexicon
Essential for "flatting" (shared housing) and socializing.

### Phrases & Idioms
- 31. **Sweet as**: Cool, awesome, or "no problem."
- 32. **Choice**: Excellent / Great.
- 33. **Chur**: Thanks / Cheers.
- 34. **Yeah, nah**: A polite "no" or a way to express hesitation.
- 35. **No worries**: You're welcome / It's fine.
- 36. **She'll be right**: It will be okay (the classic Kiwi optimistic attitude).
- 37. **Knackered**: Very tired or exhausted.
- 38. **Munted**: Broken, ruined, or damaged.
- 39. **Stoked**: Very happy or excited.
- 40. **Hard out**: To agree strongly.

### Objects & Daily Life
- 41. **Jandals**: Flip-flops / Rubber sandals.
- 42. **Togs**: Swimsuit.
- 43. **Chilly bin**: A portable cooler.
- 44. **Dairy**: A local convenience store.
- 45. **Bach** *(pronounced 'batch')*: A holiday home.
- 46. **Tramping**: Hiking or trekking.
- 47. **Gumboots**: Waterproof rubber boots.
- 48. **Flatting**: Living in a shared rental house.
- 49. **Suss**: To figure something out.
- 50. **Wop-wops**: Out in the middle of nowhere.

## 🌿 3. Te Reo Māori in Everyday Use
New Zealand is bicultural. These words are used daily in official and social contexts.

- 61. **Kia ora**: Hello / Thank you.
- 62. **Aotearoa**: New Zealand (Land of the Long White Cloud).
- 63. **Whānau**: Family / Close-knit group.
- 64. **Kai**: Food.
- 65. **Mana**: Prestige, authority, or status.
- 66. **Mahi**: Work or activity.
- 67. **Marae**: Communal meeting place for Māori.
- 68. **Pākehā**: New Zealanders of European descent.
- 69. **Haere mai**: Welcome.
- 70. **Ka pai**: Good / Well done.
- 71. **Iwi**: Tribe or people group.
- 72. **Waiata**: Song.
- 73. **Karakia**: Prayer or opening ritual.
- 74. **Hāngī**: Traditional feast cooked in an earth oven.
- 75. **Koha**: A gift or contribution.

## 🎓 4. Campus & Administrative Terms
Common terminology used by NZ universities (UoA, Vic, Otago, etc.).

- 81. **Trimester/Semester**: Academic terms.
- 82. **Lecture/Tutorial**: Class formats.
- 83. **Plagiarism**: Using others' work without credit (serious offense).
- 84. **Reference**: Citing sources (often APA style in NZ).
- 85. **Extension**: Extra time for an assignment.
- 86. **Orientation (O-Week)**: First week events.
- 87. **Halls of Residence**: On-campus student housing.
- 88. **GPA**: Grade Point Average.
- 89. **Alumni**: Former students.
- 90. **Compulsory**: Mandatory or required.
